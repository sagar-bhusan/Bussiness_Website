About Us

Analog and Digital located in Kharagpur city is operational since 1999, we are a renowned computer centre in Kharagpur city ,we have been providing exceptional computer training & services since then.

Our journey of excellence in computer training dates back to 1999. We take pride in our students who are now flourishing in esteemed corporate IT roles around the world.

Our commitment to delivering high-quality services is a testament to our dedication. We understand the importance of keeping up with the rapidly evolving technology landscape, and we strive to equip our students with the skills they need to excel.

At Analog and Digital, we believe that our reward lies in the satisfaction of our clients and students. With a legacy built on trust and expertise, we continue to shape the future of IT professionals and contribute to the growth of the industry.
